Samsung account Privacy Notice


Effective [January 16, 2024]

At Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd. (“Samsung”), we know how important privacy is to our customers. We created this Privacy Notice to make sure you understand how we collect and use your personal information for Samsung account and its services and features, as listed below and described in this Privacy Notice (collectively, our “Services”).

Our Privacy Policy at https://account.samsung.com/membership/terms/privacypolicy also applies to your use of our Services. Our Privacy Policy contains more information about how we use your data. It also includes information about your rights and how to contact us. Please read our Privacy Policy in addition to this Privacy Notice. However, this Privacy Notice shall always prevail over the Privacy Policy in relation to how we use your information for our Services.
Samsung account is your one-stop personal account used to access and enjoy some of our Samsung Services. These Services include:


Samsung account

Some Samsung apps and services (such as Samsung Cloud and Samsung Hub) require a Samsung account. With a Samsung account, you can enjoy many of these services and apps. In addition, certain third-party apps and services may allow you to link your Samsung account with that third-party account for easier log-in or enhanced features (please refer to the “Account and Service Linking” section below).


Samsung Hub

• Access to free and live TV : You can watch TV on your Samsung Smart TV and enjoy access to news, sports and entertainment.
• Streaming Apps : Access to apps to stream content on your Samsung Smart TV.


Sync and Back-Up Features:

• Sync and back up data : You can back up or sync your data on your mobile devices (using Samsung Cloud) and Smart TVs.
• Call & text on other devices : Enjoy seamless communication by syncing text messages in the Messages app and phone calls on your secondary mobile Galaxy devices when logged into the same Samsung account.


Membership Benefits

• Save Payment Information : Save your credit card so you can enjoy easier checkout when making purchases across various Samsung services, apps and websites.
• Samsung Rewards : Accumulate rewards points when making purchases of participating Samsung services and products.
• Samsung Members App : You can connect and interact with other Samsung account users on our social media platforms, such as our Samsung Members Community. Here, you can communicate with other Samsung account users on our online forum and register your owned products, as well as contact us through our customer communication channel.
• Group Sharing and Quick Share : Easily share files with other Samsung account users.
• Samsung Visit-In : Receive location-based marketing promotions and offers from participating Samsung and third-party stores near you.


Security Services

• Samsung Pass : Store your log-in information and contact information for easier log-in or check out.
• SmartThings Find/Samsung Find: Locate devices such as your phone, wearables, and tablet with the help of SmartThings Find/Samsung Find, as well as share the locations of your devices with other Samsung account users.
• Secure Wi-Fi : Protect transmitted data on public networks.
• Digital Key : Store participating keys (such as home or building entrance or car keys) on your phone.
• Knox Matrix : Securely store your keys used by some Samsung services on the server based on a PIN, password or pattern set on your lock screen.


Advanced intelligence

Get intelligence features within certain apps or services. Check out the advanced intelligence section below for more details.


Voice Assistant

Once you create a Samsung account, on compatible devices, you can easily control your device by voice command using Bixby Voice. You can activate Bixby Voice by pressing the invoke button on your device or by saying “Hi, Bixby.” The status indicator on your device, such as an on-screen indicator or flashing LED, lets you know when Bixby Voice is activated. Please see the Voice Assistant section below to see the types of data Bixby Voice collects.


WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

When you create a Samsung account with us, we ask for information to help you get set up, such as your name, nickname, date of birth, gender, phone number, password and email address.

In addition, when you contact customer service about our Services, your communications, including any information you provide regarding your identity and the product or service you’re contacting us about, will be collected.

The Chatbot service functionality on our website is operated by a third party service provider that also has access to your name, email and phone number, in addition to the information that you provide to an agent when the Chatbot functionality is activated by you. As a result, the Chatbot agents can provide you with a more integrated customer service experience during your chat session.

Once you set up your Samsung account and log in, you can use various convenient features on your devices, such as the Services mentioned above. You can see the features available to you in the Samsung account settings. When using Samsung account and the Services, we collect and maintain information about you as detailed below.

Information While Using Samsung account and our Services

In order for you to use our Services, certain information needs to be collected by us, as explained below.


Smart Hub

For details on the information collected through Smart Hub, please see our Smart Hub Privacy Notice at https://smarthub.termsnprivacy.com/terms/CA_en.html.


Sync and Back-up Features:

• Sync and back up data : You can sync, back up and restore your data on mobile devices and Smart TVs. For example, you can choose to back up or sync certain data to our cloud service (such as Samsung Cloud for your mobile devices). The data you choose to back up or sync will automatically be sent to and stored by Samsung. These data may include downloaded apps, Call and Text history, your Contacts list, appointment and alert settings you have saved (such as Calendar, Notes, Clock and Reminder entries), your Gallery and Bookmarks on Samsung Internet. For Smart TVs, these data may include post code and broadcast information (such as service provider, channel information and antenna type), device, Smart TV (including PIN) and Samsung account profile information, settings and configurations. For Gallery sync, this feature is offered through Microsoft OneDrive. If you turn on syncing on one device where you are logged into your Samsung account, sync will automatically turn on in your other devices where you have logged into your Samsung account. You can manage your sync preferences in the Samsung account settings in any of your devices.
• Call and text on other devices : By enabling Call & text on other devices, we collect information such as Contacts and call and message logs so you can experience multi-device communication by letting you send or receive calls or messages on your secondary device(s). Your messages and calls will also need to be collected to sync that data to your secondary device(s).


Membership Benefits:

• Save Payment Information : When you register your credit card to your Samsung account for easier payment options, we collect information such as credit card number, name, and expiration date. Also, when you save information, like your address for faster checkout when you make purchases on your mobile device, Smart TV or Samsung websites, we collect the information you choose to submit.
• Samsung Rewards : By having a Samsung account, various coupons, discounts and gift cards are available to you, when you choose to avail of this feature. You are also eligible to receive rewards points when purchasing various Samsung products and services. The rewards available to you, including points, and how and where you spend or redeem them are collected by us.
• Samsung Members App : Content you provide through or on the Samsung Members Community, such as your profile information, posts and comments (including media you upload) and information on the products you register (including warranty information), as well as communication (including information you submit) with our customer team and other Samsung Members Community members (including the content of personal messages) is collected.
• Group Sharing : Group Sharing allows you to easily send or share files and other information, such as contacts, profile information, notes, reminders, and calendar schedules to other Samsung account users, as well as displaying profile images in your contacts list and when you receive calls. We collect the media and information you choose to send, as well as your contacts and profile information (including profile information of your contacts). Other Samsung sharing features which are not a part of Samsung account will also use your contacts and profile information to allow you to share media and information through those features.
When you join a group and share media and files (such as when you join a group to share notes, reminders, calendar entries, or gallery media), these entries will be visible to all other members of the group. You can delete or edit gallery, calendar, and reminder entries at any time while you are a member of the group. For shared note entries, you may edit and delete entries if granted permission by the group leader. Other members may be able to edit or delete your shared entry.
If you leave or are removed from a group, your shared entries which have not been deleted will remain in the group for other members to see and edit.
• Quick Share: The Quick Share feature, available on certain Galaxy devices, enables you to quickly share photos, videos, and files. Quick Share can be used to share content between Galaxy devices in close proximity to each other via the device-to-device method. Quick Share can also be used to share content by sending a link that can be used to access the shared content using an internet-connected device. When using this method, we collect the content you share, which will remain available for 3 days before it is automatically deleted. You can also add extra protection such as file encryption, by using the private sharing option, which allows you to set an expiration date and revoke access to your content. By using this feature, the content you share with a recipient will be deleted from our server immediately after the recipient receives and downloads it. Please note that content sent by private sharing will be held on an encrypted server for up to 7 days until either the recipient downloads the content, or the 7 days expires, whichever is earlier.
• Samsung Visit-In : When you are near participating Samsung or third-party stores offering benefits or coupons, information about the store offering benefits or coupons will be collected. With your separate consent, your device’s location information, such as GPS signal or information about nearby Wi-Fi access points and cell towers, is sent to us when you choose to enable this feature.


Security Services:

• Samsung Pass : When you save your information (e.g., name, phone number, email, mailing or shipping address), you can easily fill in and use for various participating Samsung or third-party apps, websites or services. Also, you can choose to back up user IDs & passwords, which enables you to log in using biometric authentication to those apps, websites or services without having to remember your ID or password. The number of successful and unsuccessful authentication attempts is also collected. Biometric information is only stored on your device and Samsung does not collect or have access to this information.
• SmartThings Find/Samsung Find: SmartThings Find/Samsung Find allows you to locate your devices when you consent to the processing of location information, including lost devices that are not connected to a network. When you request the location of any of your devices logged in to your Samsung account or other Samsung devices that connect to your Samsung account (such as your wearable device), and if the device is turned on and you have agreed to Samsung’s processing of your location information, the location of each device is sent to Samsung to show your device(s) on a map. In certain instances (such as when you want to share the location of your device with other users when using Samsung SmartThings), SmartThings Find/Samsung Find allows you to share your device’s location with other Samsung account users.


SmartThings Find/Samsung Find also has several optional features which you can turn on, as described below.

i. Send Last Location : Your device’s last location will be automatically sent to Samsung whenever your device’s battery reaches a critically low level (5% or lower) to help you find the device even after it’s turned off.
ii. Offline Finding: This feature will activate if your device remains disconnected from its mobile or Wi-Fi network after a certain period of time. You will be able to locate it using Bluetooth with the help of other nearby Samsung devices, which also have Offline Finding enabled. By enabling this feature, your device will also help others find their lost devices around you by using your device’s location. No information about you will be visible to others, nor will information about others be visible to you. Your device, together with other enabled devices, will simply create a Bluetooth network to work together and locate the lost device(s).
• SmartThings Find : SmartThings Find may require you to register for Samsung SmartThings. For more information about how Samsung SmartThings processes your information, please review the Samsung SmartThings Privacy Notice.
• Secure Wi-Fi : Data transmitted from your device when using a public network, for example at a coffee shop or airport terminal, will have an extra layer of security during its transit. Secure Wi-Fi offers various subscription plans depending on the amount of data you need protected. When you do subscribe to one of the Secure Wi-Fi subscription plans, information will be collected related to that subscription plan (such as which product purchased and how and when the product was purchased).
• Digital Key : Information related to the keys are collected, such as the manufacturer, device information, and key information.
• Knox Matrix : Store your keys used in some Samsung services securely on the server and make them accessible only on authenticated device(s). It extracts authentication information by processing a PIN, pattern, or password set on the lock screen. This authentication information is collected and cannot infer the PIN, pattern, or password.

For example, some of the advanced intelligence services may provide translations or summaries for text you provide access to, summaries of your calls, or may provide spelling or grammar correction or style recommendations for you. You can check which apps and services offer advanced intelligence by going into your Samsung account settings and clicking the advanced intelligence tab.

Some of the advanced intelligence services can process your data inputs solely within your device. Other advanced intelligence services require you to be signed into your Samsung account and to consent to the processing of your data outside of your device, such as your account information, device and app information, and your data inputs (including text, voice or media files), to produce results.
